// PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Welcome to Afflux. Your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, process, and protect your information as an MVP platform designed for skill development and verified achievements.

1. Information We Collect

To operate our gamified metrics, tracking features, and platform services, we collect:

  • Account Credentials: Full name, username, email address, and encrypted password strings.
  • Profile Information: Biography details and linked social media accounts (GitHub, LinkedIn, Website, Instagram, YouTube) that you voluntarily add.
  • Gamification & System Data: Earned experience points (XP), Level progression metrics, login history (including 7th and 30th milestones), and leaderboard rankings.
  • Event Logs: Comprehensive event results, display names for participation, and system records of issued certificate codes.

2. How We Process Data

Your records are processed securely to execute core platform features, including:

  • Managing dynamic profile views and processing administrative profile adjustments.
  • Calculating correct platform-wide Leaderboard placements.
  • Issuing verifiable digital certificates matching your custom input display name.
  • Awarding automated XP incentives (e.g., the 500 XP profile completion bonus or daily sign-in milestones).

3. Public Transparency Data

To foster healthy competition, specific parameters remain public by default on profiles and leaderboards:

  • Public identity parameters: Name, username, bio fields, and active social links.
  • Gamification stats: Current Level standing, total XP count, structural badge sets, and relative leaderboard position.

Note: Your raw email addresses, administrative identifiers, and encrypted password entries are never exposed publicly.

4. Immutable Certificate Records

Certificates generated via Afflux represent official completion events. To guarantee long-term legitimacy for external checkers, unique certificate codes remain publicly queryable and verifiable, even if an individual modifies their profile name or requests account deletion.

5. Security & Framework

We leverage industrial cryptography methods (such as salted hashing via `werkzeug.security`) to prevent raw password exposure. While we match secure framework standard operating models, please recognize that no web platform provides absolute bulletproof transmission protection.

6. Core Rights & Inquiries

You preserve the right to edit profile metrics, adjust connected links, or update profile entries directly via your dashboard configuration panel. For specialized queries regarding database deletion configurations, please contact the Afflux administration desk.
